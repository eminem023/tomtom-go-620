How to Update TomTom GO 620 Maps

Keeping your TomTom GO 620 GPS updated ensures you always get accurate directions, real-time traffic updates, and access to the latest points of interest. This guide walks you through simple steps to update your GO 620 maps effortlessly.

Why Update Your TomTom GO 620?

  • Access the latest road changes and detours
  • Get up-to-date traffic alerts and route planning
  • Improve GPS performance and functionality
  • Stay safe with updated speed limits and zones

Update via Wi-Fi (No Computer Needed)

  • Turn on your GO 620 device
  • Connect it to a secure Wi-Fi network
  • Tap Settings → Updates & New Items
  • The device will automatically check for updates
  • Download and install all available map and software updates

Update via MyDrive Connect (Using USB)

  • Download and install MyDrive Connect on your PC or Mac
  • Connect your GO 620 to the computer using a USB cable
  • Launch MyDrive Connect and log in
  • The dashboard will display available updates
  • Select the updates and click Install
  • Keep your device connected until the update completes

Troubleshooting TomTom GO 620 Update Issues

  • Ensure strong and stable internet or Wi-Fi
  • Charge your device or keep it plugged in
  • Restart the device if it freezes during the update
  • Update MyDrive Connect if it’s outdated

Conclusion

Map updates for your TomTom GO 620 are crucial to reliable and smooth navigation. With free lifetime map updates, it’s easy to stay current—whether you choose the Wi-Fi method or use a PC with MyDrive Connect. Safe travels!
